About me

Hello, My name is Shraddha Pandhe. Currently I am working as a Graduate Research Assistant in WINLAB, Rutgers University . I am also a Masters Thesis student at Rutgers University. Currently I am working on a very interesting project, Wireless Cloud Computing. In this ongoing project, I am trying to build a cloud on locally, abundantly available smart handheld device. I finished a part of this project during my summer internship in WINLAB.

Before my summer internship, I worked as a Teaching Assistant at Rutgers University. In course Digital Logic Design, I conducted lab sessions for apprx 60 students. Never knew before that teaching could be so much fun! In another semester, I conducted recitations and problem review sessions for the course Principles of Communication Systems for a class of 35 students.

Before I joined Rutgers as a Masters Student, I worked with Cisco Systems Inc, as a Software Engineer from July 2009 to July 2011. I handled billing requirements of over 60 GGSN customers. I developed a tool for parsing millions of CDRs (Charging Data Record). It goes through the record, field-by-field, and highlights any unexpected behavior. My job responsibilities also included analyzing the billing requirements of customers, and creating a Gap Analysis document, which highlights the gaps in existing implementation and requirement. To achieve this, I had to collaborate with many other teams for additional inputs and review comments. Overall, work experience in Cisco was very enriching.

Apart from all these, I like organizing and managing stuff. During my undergrad, I organized a bunch of technical and non-technical events, involving viewership of ~7000 people! The event I enjoyed the most was Regatta 2008. We also made a video, The Making of Regatta: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JZkposf6tlM&feature=related.

As you would have guessed, I love Rowing! In fact, I won all possible awards that COEP has to offer, for a good rower, including Best Outgoing Sports Person! I know I am boasting, but I am really proud of it :)

Well, I guess this is it for now! Checkout my projects in Projects.
